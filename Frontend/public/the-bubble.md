# The Bubble

The Bubble is Cribb's community content hub — student-authored posts about events, deals, news, lifestyle, and food in Guelph.

## Who can post

- Verified University of Guelph students can request write access
- Approved external writers (local creators, student journalists) can apply and be approved by Cribb

## Categories

- **Events** — campus, club, and city events with dates and locations
- **Deals** — student discounts, free food, sales at Guelph businesses
- **News** — what's happening in the student community
- **Lifestyle** — student life in Guelph
- **Food** — restaurant reviews and food finds
- **Other** — anything else useful to students

## Post structure

Every post has a title, cover image, preview snippet, category tag, and full body. Event posts include date, location, and a link. Deal posts include an expiry date.
