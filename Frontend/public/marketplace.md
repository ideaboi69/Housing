# Student marketplace

The Cribb marketplace is a buy-sell-free board limited to verified University of Guelph students. It's designed for the kinds of things students actually trade — furniture, textbooks, kitchenware, electronics, decor, bikes, and household goods.

## Categories

Furniture, textbooks, kitchenware, electronics, decor, clothing, sports gear, bikes, school supplies, and free-to-give-away items.

## Listing types

- **Fixed price** — sell at a set price
- **Negotiable** — set a price, accept offers
- **Free** — give away items you no longer need

## Item details

Every item shows photos (up to 5), condition (new, like new, good, fair, poor), pricing, pickup location, pickup notes, and an in-app messaging thread between buyer and seller.

## Safety

- Only students with verified @uoguelph.ca emails can list or buy
- Cribb does not process payments — buyers and sellers arrange that directly
- Meet in safe public locations for pickup
- Flag suspicious or scam listings via the in-app report button

## Pricing

Free to use. No commission, no listing fees, no premium tiers.
