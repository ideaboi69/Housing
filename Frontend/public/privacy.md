# Privacy Policy

Cribb is operated by Adeoluwa Ojulari and David Amaefula as individuals. We follow PIPEDA and applicable Ontario privacy law.

## What we collect

- Account info: name, @uoguelph.ca email, password (hashed), profile photo, year, program, bio
- Landlord verification: government ID, proof of ownership, phone, company name
- Listings, sublets, marketplace items, posts you create
- Roommate quiz answers used only for matching
- Direct messages between users
- Usage data: IP, browser, device info
- Error data via Sentry for debugging

## How we use it

To run your account, match listings and roommates, verify landlords, send transactional email, prevent fraud, and debug issues. We do not sell personal data. We do not run ads. We do not share with data brokers.

## AI features

The AI listing comparison feature sends listing data (rent, amenities, location, verification status, Cribb score) to Anthropic to generate the comparison. No names, emails, or messages are sent. Anthropic does not train on it.

## Service providers

Neon (database), Render (hosting), Cloudinary (images), AWS S3 and Textract (verification documents), Resend (email), Cloudflare (Turnstile bot protection), Sentry (error monitoring), Anthropic (AI compare), Upstash (caching), Google Maps (maps).

## Your rights

Under PIPEDA you can access, correct, or delete your data, and withdraw consent. Email professionaldavid14@gmail.com or adeoluwaojulari@gmail.com.

## Retention

Account data is kept while your account is active. Deletion removes personal data within 30 days. Verification documents are deleted within 90 days of account deletion.

## Security

bcrypt password hashing, HTTPS everywhere, encrypted S3, rate-limited auth, Cloudflare Turnstile on signup.
