# Cribb for landlords

Cribb lets landlords reach verified University of Guelph students directly. There are no listing fees, no commissions, and no per-message charges.

## How to get started

1. Sign up at https://findyourcribb.com/landlord/signup
2. Upload a government-issued ID and proof of property ownership (deed, property tax document, or utility bill in your name)
3. Documents are processed through AWS Textract and reviewed
4. Once approved, you get a verification badge and can publish listings

## What you can post

- One or more properties
- Multiple listings per property (e.g., per-room rentals)
- Photos, rent breakdown, amenities, lease terms, move-in date, gender preference, sublet status

## Pricing

Free. Cribb does not collect rent, hold deposits, or charge transaction fees. All rent and deposit arrangements happen directly between you and the student.

## Communication

Students can message you directly through the platform. You receive an email notification for new messages.

## Viewings

Cribb includes a built-in viewing system. Set your availability and students book a 1-hour slot in one click. Both parties get a 24-hour email reminder before the viewing.
