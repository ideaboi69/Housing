# Browse listings

The browse page on Cribb is where University of Guelph students search for off-campus housing in Guelph.

## What's on every listing

- Photos and a full description
- Rent breakdown — per-room pricing or total rent
- Cribb Score (0–100) combining property data and tenant reviews
- Amenities: furnishing, parking, laundry, utilities, Wi-Fi, A/C, dishwasher, gym, balcony, accessibility
- Distance to the University of Guelph, walk time, drive time, bus time, nearest bus route
- Lease type (8-month, 12-month, flexible) and move-in date
- Identity-verified landlord badge

## Filters

Students can filter by price range, property type (house, apartment, townhouse, room), furnishing, parking, laundry, utilities included, distance to campus, lease type, sublet status, gender preference, and move-in date.

## How verification works

Only landlords who have uploaded government ID and proof of property ownership can publish listings. Verification documents are processed through AWS Textract and reviewed before the landlord can post anything. Verified landlords display a badge on every listing.

## Pricing

Browsing is free. Cribb does not collect rent, hold deposits, or charge listing or transaction fees.
