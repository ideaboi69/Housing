# Sublets on Cribb

The sublet board on Cribb is for University of Guelph students who need to fill or find short-term housing — a summer, a co-op term, a semester abroad, or any partial-term rental.

## What you can post

- Any duration, from a single month to a full term
- Furnished or unfurnished
- Per-room or whole-unit sublets
- Photos, amenities, rent, exact start and end dates
- Distance to campus and nearest bus route

## What's on every sublet

- Start and end dates of the sublet period
- Monthly rent and what's included (utilities, Wi-Fi)
- Room type (private, shared, studio, etc.)
- Photos and a description of the space
- Amenities and accessibility info
- Direct messaging with the student posting

## Who can post

Any current University of Guelph student with a verified @uoguelph.ca email. Students subletting from a landlord must have permission from their landlord where required by their lease.

## Cost

Free to post, free to browse. Cribb does not take a cut of the sublet rent and does not handle deposits or payment.

## Viewings

Sublet listings include a built-in viewing booking system. Posters set their availability and inquiring students book a 1-hour slot in one click. Both parties get email reminders 24 hours before the viewing.
