# Roommate groups on Cribb

Cribb's roommate feature is built around the reality of how most students actually find housing — with friends. It's not stranger matching.

## How groups work

- Create a group with a name, description, and the number of spots needed
- Invite friends with their @uoguelph.ca email
- Browse other students who are also looking for groups
- Send and receive invites
- Group owners can manage members and link a verified landlord listing

## Compatibility quiz

Students fill out a 10-factor compatibility quiz covering:

1. Sleep schedule
2. Cleanliness
3. Noise level
4. Guests
5. Study habits
6. Smoking
7. Pets
8. Kitchen use
9. Budget range
10. Move-in timing

Cleanliness, sleep schedule, and noise level are weighted most heavily because they're the most common sources of roommate conflict.

## Group photos and visibility

Groups can upload a group photo and choose visibility. Group owners control who can join.

## Landlord invite links

Group owners can generate a one-click invite link for a specific landlord. The landlord clicks the link, completes verification, and creates a property/listing tied to the group. Useful for groups that already have a house in mind.

## Pricing

Free for students and landlords.
